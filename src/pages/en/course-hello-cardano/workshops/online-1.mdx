# Online Workshop #1

**May 28, 2025** | 24 attendees

The first online workshop in the second milestone of our Cardano Project Catalyst Fund13 proposal, providing online meetups and materials to support builders in the Cardano ecosystem.

Hosted with Blazar Labs and led by Jingles (founder of Mesh), this session introduced MeshJS and the Mesh SDK to both newcomers and experienced Cardano developers. The workshop covered high-level architecture, specific SDK features, and concluded with an open Q&A.
